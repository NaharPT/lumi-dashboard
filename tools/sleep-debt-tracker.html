Sleep Hygiene Tips

  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule, even on weekends
  • Create a dark, cool, quiet sleeping environment (65-68°F / 18-20°C)
  • Avoid screens 1 hour before bed (blue light disrupts melatonin)
  • Limit caffeine after 2 PM and alcohol before bed
  • Exercise regularly, but not within 3 hours of bedtime
  • Use bed only for sleep and intimacy, not work or TV
  • If you can't sleep after 20 minutes, get up and do a quiet activity
